## Case study intro

AI platforms change constantly, but the updates are scattered across changelogs, release notes, documentation hubs, and developer portals. SignalBrief was designed to turn that scattered update stream into a controlled, reviewable newsletter workflow without relying on broad scraping or unsupported AI summaries.

## Technical summary

The app uses SQLite as the local memory layer for sources, scan runs, snapshots, detected changes, and generated newsletter drafts. Source content is fetched server-side, cleaned, saved locally, compared against prior snapshots, and converted into reviewable newsletter items.

## Product principle

Do not automate distribution until the content can be trusted.
